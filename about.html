About

How to Play

  • Select a game mode, either 6x6 or 10x10
  • The completed board will be shown for 5 seconds. Try to remember which squares have which images.
  • Click two squares to reveal them. If they match, they will stay revealed. If they don't match, they will flip over again after a second and you will gain an error. Hitting 10 errors will end the game.
  • Match every square to complete the board and get a new one. You have ten minutes to complete as many as you can. Only completed boards count towards your score.
